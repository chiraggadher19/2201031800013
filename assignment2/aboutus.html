about us

Our team at PETDOME are dedicated in making this world a better place for helpless animals.

Welcome to PETDOME, where we connect loving homes with deserving pets in need. Our mission is to provide a platform that makes pet adoption accessible, convenient, and fulfilling for both animals and adopters.

how the journey was started?

PETDOME was founded with a passion for animals and a commitment to making difference in their lives. the belif that every pet deserves a loving home and every home deserves a loyal companion.

Support: We provide ongoing support and resources to adopters, helping them seamlessly transition their new pet into their home and providing guidance on pet care, training, and behavior.
